Overview

During my 7-month internship at Inspirame in Summer and Fall 2023, I worked as a Product Design Intern focusing on TecoAdmin, a platform for business partners and school administrators. My role involved researching, designing, and prototyping analytics and management tools. Collaborating closely with my mentor, I contributed to the development of project specs and set design goals, starting with audits and initial research, and moving into designing a task management system and building a comprehensive design system.

Experience

I led user testing sessions with my interns and incorporated feedback to improve the designs. Throughout the internship, I learned invaluable lessons about the product development life cycle, research planning, feedback analysis, and the importance of exploring multiple design ideas before finalizing solutions.

Learnings

This internship taught be a lot about the product development life cycle, asking the right questions, how to plan research studies and analyze feedback, and to explore as much ideas as you can before settling.

Please note that all of my work during this internship is under NDA, so feel free to contact me directly for further details.
